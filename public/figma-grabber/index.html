How to install

Three steps. Under a minute.

Show your bookmarks bar

In Chrome, press ⌘ Shift B (Mac) or Ctrl Shift B (Windows) to reveal it.

Drag the button to your bookmarks bar

Click and hold the purple Figma Grabber button above, drag it up to your bookmarks bar, and release. Or right-click it and choose Bookmark this link.

You're done

The bookmarklet is saved. You won't need to come back here — it's yours now, tied to no account or login.

How to use

One click from any page.

Navigate to any page

Open the site or UI you want to bring into Figma. Works on any page you can view in Chrome.

Click Figma Grabber

A toolbar appears at the bottom of the page. Choose Select element to pick a specific component, Entire screen for the full viewport, or Copy to clipboard for the whole page immediately.

Paste into Figma

Your clipboard now holds a Figma-ready JSON payload. Paste it into your Figma MCP workflow. Nothing leaves your machine — no API calls, no server, just your clipboard.
